About Gigstarter
https://www.gigstarter.nl

Online marketplace to find and book live music

How it works ...

Bookers can use the SEARCH page to find and contact bands directly

OR

Place a Call ( Oproep ) to Gigstarter

What happens after placing a call?

  • Employees at gigstarter receive the booking call request
  • Manually go through the database to find the appropriate band or artists
  • Send an email to the artists with contact info of the booker
  • If the artist is available they can directly get in touch with the booker.

Problem statement

  • Placing a call is a time consuming process for the employees, and the booker has to wait sometimes for few days
  • Bookers are not participating in selecting the bands
  • The selection may not be acurate

How does Muziek Match address the problem?

Increase engagement by providing a fun and interactive ‘Tinder-like’ experience for the users

Shortening the amount of time it takes and increase accuracy to find the right band/artists

About Muziek Match Web App
https://bitbucket.org/onetoughcookie226/gs-app

Web application made in reactjs to find and book live music for events

How it works?

User can go through the list faster and place a call request directly

Eliminating the extra time that had to wait until gigstarter employee manually answers to the call and makes the matches

Why React?

  • Personal goal to learn React
  • Most popular so easier to transfer knowledge
  • Modern high performance framework
  • Overall good fit for the project
